# Customer Personas

This page helps teams quickly identify where they fit and which questions matter first.

## 1) Executive Buyer / Sponsor

**Primary concern:** Time-to-value, risk, and investment clarity.

- "What business outcome can we realize in 90 days?"
- "Can we start small and scale without redoing architecture?"
- "How does this reduce delivery and compliance risk?"

- [Sales One-Pager](sales-one-pager.md)

## 2) CTO / Platform Owner

**Primary concern:** Deployability, architecture fit, and operational model.

- "Can we deploy only the components we need?"
- "How does this integrate with existing data and workflow platforms?"
- "What are the runtime dependencies and environment constraints?"

Start with:

- [Deployment and Integration Paths](deployment-integration-paths.md)

## 3) Data / Bioinformatics / MLOps Lead

**Primary concern:** Pipeline compatibility, reproducibility, and quality controls.

- "Can HELIOS wrap existing Nextflow/Snakemake work without re-platforming?"
- "How do we retain traceability from input to result?"
- "How can we make RAG on internal datasets safe and useful?"

**Primary concern:** Evidence quality, documentation, and audit readiness.

- "How does Ferrum + HELIOS help for EHDS-oriented requirements?"
- "What is evidence tooling vs what remains organizational responsibility?"
- "Can we produce defensible technical documentation from real runs?"

- "How do I inspect generated artifacts and reports?"
